About Us

Madha Engineering College is a one-campus school for engineering affiliated to Anna University and is a co-educational engineering college located in Kunrathur, Chennai, India. It was founded in 1998. The College is one of the institutions belonging to the Madha Group of Educational Institutions.

ZEPHYR is an esteemed National Level Technical Symposium hosted by the Department of Computer Science and Engineering (CSE).Join us at ZEPHYR, where the spirit of innovation takes flight, and together, we shape the future of computer science and engineering.
